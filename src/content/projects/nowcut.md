## Overview

NowCut brings professional barbers on-demand to customers' homes and offices. At IOMechs, I made significant contributions to the **Admin Portal** and the **Shop Portal** — the operational backbone of the platform used by the business and its barber network.

## My Contributions

- **Admin Portal** — Contributed extensively to the internal management dashboard covering barber onboarding and verification, booking oversight, payment reconciliation, dispute handling, and platform-wide analytics
- **Shop Portal** — Contributed to the barber-facing dashboard for managing personal schedules, viewing upcoming and past appointments, tracking earnings, and handling customer requests
